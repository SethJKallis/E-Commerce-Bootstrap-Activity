Dark Souls I

Dark Souls I

The first installment in a series of action role-playing games created by Hidetaka Miyazaki of FromSoftware. The game was published by Bandai Namco in 2011.
Dark Souls I is often cited as one of the greatest video games.
The series had received both praise and criticism due to their high difficulty.

Dark Souls II

Dark Souls II

The second installment in a series of action role-playing games created by Hidetaka Miyazaki of FromSoftware. The game was published by Bandai Namco in 2014.
It was released on PC, PlayStation and Xbox.
The setting of the second Souls game is the kingdom of Drangleic.
The second installment features both PvE and PvP game-modes which give the game tons of replay-ability.

Dark Souls III

Dark Souls III

The final, and arguably the best, installment in a series of action role-playing games created by Hidetaka Miyazaki of FromSoftware. The game was published by Bandai Namco in 2016.
Over 3 million copies of the game were sold worldwide within the first 2 months, and 10 million copies had been sold by 2020.
Dark Souls III also has some of the franchise's best downloadable content, such as Ashes of Ariandel and The Ringed City.
No one plays a Souls game and comes out the same afterwards...
